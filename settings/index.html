💳 Payments

Receive money from customers and pay for services without ever leaving Confluo. Here is how it works:

  1. Choose a payment provider below and connect your account.
  2. Each provider passes an independent security review before going live.
  3. Once live, sales from your chats and posts settle directly to your bank or wallet.
  • 🏦Paystack · Nigeria & AfricaAccept cards, bank transfers, and USSD payments from customers across Africa.Security review in progress
  • 🌍Stripe · InternationalAccept international debit and credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay worldwide.Security review in progress
  • 🪙Crypto (USDC) · BorderlessSend and receive stablecoin payments with no borders and low fees.Security review in progress

🔒 Your money is protected. Confluo never stores your card number, bank login, or wallet keys. All payment details are handled by the certified providers above over encrypted connections, and every payout is recorded in a tamper-proof ledger you can download at any time.
